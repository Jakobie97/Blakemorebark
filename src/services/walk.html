Walk

Tail Waggin’ Walking Services

Take a stroll with the BlakemoreBark! We offer a variety of different walks perfect for you pups needs. Even when it is just a quick around the block rendevous or an expodition your pup will enjoy the adventure.

Promenade to The Blakemore Bark

The Blakemoore Rendezvous

Before The Blakemoore Bark will accept a new client, we recommend that one of our trained stewards meet with the potential new client and their parent(s). This is so the animal and parent may feel more comfortable with the steward handling your services, and experience with our company. Due to our staff’s schedule, the initial “Blakemoore Rendezvous” has fee that may be charged due to distance or a pup with a distinct scheduling need.

Note: *general assessments are scheduled in 15-30 min. sessions

The Blakemoore Strut

For our parents who find a scheduling conflict, we are there for you four-legged friend. This service is a simple 10-20 minute walk, it’s sole purpose is to allow for your pet to eliminate outside the house and to simply allow them to stretch their legs, while getting some fresh air outside.

The Blakemoore Jaunt

This services is for our more energetic companions who need longer walks. “The Jaunt” entails an hour-long walk, and guarantees at least one elimination. Longer walks are available with notice.

The Blakemoore Affair

Looking to socialize your new pup, or looking for a friend for a long-time companion? This is the option for you! We can arrange for your pet and another local pet in your area looking for the same sort of friendship and bond for continued play.

The Blakemoore Adventure

The Blakemoore Adventure is when two pups from our ‘Blakemoore Affair’ program require a steward to maintain decorum amongst our two new friends. The time frame for this engagement is between 45-60 minutes including a walk, to allow for elimination. Arrangements will be needed as to which owners’ home the friends will meet. Transportation available if needed.

Note: *An adventure begins after the initial “Blakemoore Affair” introduction.

The Blakemoore Expedition

For our popular pups, who love a little more attention from other four legged buddies. The Blakemoore Bark will arrange for a trip to the nearest dog park to your home, or the dog park of your preference.

Note: any paper work need to attend said park will need to filed with the organization before hand.

*Before our expedition begins we will need to pickup your companion. This service is 60 minutes, which is not inclusive of travel time from your home. Additional time can be arranged with notice, for more than 60 minutes of play. At this time, this service is only for animals in the same home or the same pick up location.
